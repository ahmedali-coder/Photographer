Q & A

A print says “sold out” in your store. Do you have anymore?

of course, but this take some time.

I ordered something from your store. Where is it and when should I get it?

It almost takes 7 days from when you placed your order to when you receive it. You will receive a tracking number via email when your order has been shipped. If you don’t see that tracking email try checking your junk mail.

Can I hire you for a project?

Sure. When sending an inquire about a potential project please include as much information as possible about it. Where are you at in the process currently? What is your timeline? Do you have a budget? The more information you send the more efficiently we’ll find out if we’re a good match to work together.

Can you do a poster for my brand?

of course, Show and tour posters only really make sense if at least 100 or more can be sold with how the financials break down of the design and screen printing cost. If you want a poster for a larger show or tour then for sure reach out.

Do you do private commissions?
